Project #2: Housekeeping with Ramed Studios

Problem: Brand Awareness

Solution: Organization and consistency are the essential pillars when it comes to brand awareness. Ramed Studios is a multi-media studio that had trouble utilizing their social media page. The issues ranged from inconsistent upload schedules to multiple pages which delayed its goal in the digital world. By developing a digital strategy that played to their strengths, it taught the studio the importance of scheduling uploads and producing content on a regular basis. As a result, the digital strategy helped with brand awareness because customers were now able to get a deeper insight behind Ramed Studios and their day to day operations.
