Project #1: Uncharted Territory with Dani Leigh

Problem: Building An Audience in Different Regions

Solution: Adaptation and providing value is key when it comes to building an audience. The LA-based artist, Dani Leigh, was performing at NYC’s first-ever Rolling Loud Festival. However New York City’s natives were unfamiliar to her sound. By adapting to the NYC subway culture, we were able to throw a live train party with NYC influencers such as KidtheWiz to rolling loud. In doing so, the experience resonated with the people of NYC and giving Dani Leigh a platform to promote herself in uncharted territory.
